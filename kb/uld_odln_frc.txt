Operational Damage Limits Notice (ODLN) for Fire Resistant Container

Location    Component   Code    Container may not be used as FRC if any of the conditions below are exceeded
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Container   All         NA      No damage that results in ADDITIONAL air flow into the container, which can be caused by punctures, tears, cracks or missing fasteners
Special Requirements:           Container has lost its fire resistant capability
                                'F' graphic symbol has to be made invalid
                                Container can still be used as standard aircraft container if damage limits for standard aircraft container are not exceeded

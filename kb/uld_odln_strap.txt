Operational Damage Limits Notice (ODLN) for Restraint Strap

Component           Strap may not be used if any of the conditions below are exceeded
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Webbing             No more than 1 cuts longer than 1 inch/25 mm
                    No cuts within 1 inches/25 mm from a stitching or other cut
Stitching           No more than 1 inch/25 mm broken stitching
Metal parts         No metal part crack or deformation larger than 1 inch/25 mm
Manufacturer Data   Attached and legible
Expiry Date         Expiry date not exceeded
